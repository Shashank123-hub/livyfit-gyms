What LivyFit handles

Built as a partner layer, not another gym program.

The experience is designed to feel natural inside a fitness community: a trainer referral, a QR code, a short intake, and a guided next step. Behind the scenes, Livy coordinates the clinical and operational pieces.

1

Gym introduces the offer

Via trainer referrals, transformation challenges, in-gym signage, email, or member events.

2

Member completes a guided intake

LivyFit captures goals, basic history, and interest areas before routing the member forward.

3

Licensed providers review eligibility

Medical decisions stay with qualified providers through Livy’s telehealth partner network.

4

Partner performance is tracked

The pilot measures lead flow, consultations, conversions, and partner revenue.

The first test
